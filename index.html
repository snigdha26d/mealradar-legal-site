About MealRadar

MealRadar helps you scan groceries and recipes, plan meals from what you already have, and waste less. It can use AI to suggest recipes and meal plans based on your pantry and preferences. You can use it as a guest, or create an account for account features.

AI suggestions are estimates and conveniences, not medical, nutritional, dietary, or allergy advice. Always review food safety and allergy suitability yourself.
